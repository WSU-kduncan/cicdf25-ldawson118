Panda bears, also known as Giant Pandas, are beloved creatures native to the mountains of central China. They are known for their distinctive black and white fur, as well as their gentle demeanor.

Learn More

Pandas primarily eat bamboo and spend most of their day resting or munching on leaves. They are an endangered species and are protected by international conservation efforts.
